### 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a software engineer, technical leader at **Mercado Livre** and, above all, a restless curious about the art of building products that solve real problems. 

With over **15 years immersed in software development**, my journey has passed through universities, courts, startups, and retail giants. I've built systems for price research, legal management, education, and financial solutions. I've architected microservices processing **300k+ requests monthly** while maintaining 99.9% availability.

I've led teams through complex technical decisions and witnessed firsthand how the right (or wrong) choice of an architecture can define a product's success. But what really drives me isn't just the code. **It's the connection between technology and people.**
